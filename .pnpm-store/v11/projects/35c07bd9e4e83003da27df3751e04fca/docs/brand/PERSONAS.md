## Conscientious Conor

Tired, thoughtful, first-time UK dad (~30s), full-time work, shares parenting. His child is past the newborn phase; the home already has toys and books. He pays for Ember Plus if it saves long research holes and helps him feel one step ahead without being patronised.

**Five tests (all must pass):** Respect · Well Duh · Stage shift · Buying judgement · Human parent  

Full persona: `web/docs/CONSCIENTIOUS_CONOR.md`

---

## Thea

Partner lens / gift and household co-parent voice used in Bible and Discover depth work. Write for a thoughtful UK parent household, not a single stereotyped shopper. Prefer inclusive “you” and “your child” unless a surface is explicitly dad- or mum-coded.

When gift mode is on, still use Writing Guidelines: positive vision, school-gate English, no crisis framing.

---

## How personas and writing guidelines work together

1. Personas decide whether the *idea* is good enough.
2. Writing Guidelines decide whether the *sentence* sounds like Ember.
3. Never use persona pain language (crisis, extremes, “falling apart”) in public copy — that was an early draft trap and is out of the system.
