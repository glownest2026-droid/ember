## Rule of thumb (strict)

- **Single (default)** — one primary purchase covers the need (toys, gear, stools, cups, balls, puzzles, dolls…).
- **Multiple (rare)** — only when parents clearly build a rotating shelf — almost always **books**.
- Owning 2–3 of something (cups, teethers) or replacing consumables does **not** make it multiple.

## Only flip to multiple if you disagree

Everything non-book is **single**. Candidates you might still want as multiple:

- Soft balls / large balls (if you treat them as a growing kit)
- Blocks / stacking sets (if you treat them as a growing kit)
- Dolls / teddies (if you treat care characters as a shelf)

Otherwise leave as single.
