# Pip's Picks: Small-world people and vehicles (34–36 months)

**Research date:** 2026-07-21 · **Researcher:** cursor · **Market:** UK · **Status:** founder-review-ready

## Educational shift

At nearly three, pretend play stretches into **whole scenarios**: someone gets in, goes somewhere, needs help, comes home. This is different from **31–33m** soft doll care (comfort/copy) and **28–30m** play kitchen (stir-and-serve). The buying job here is a **missing journey theme** — not a bigger base set.

### 1. Happyland London Bus Playset
Three passengers, a bus stop and a drivable bus with sounds — the simplest UK whole-journey starter. Chunky Happyland tier; combines with pieces already owned.

### 2. LEGO DUPLO Ambulance & Driver
Driver plus patient, ambulance with opening roof and bed — a help-and-return arc in a small box. DUPLO-sturdy; no siren (some parents expect sound).

### 3. LEGO DUPLO 3-in-1 Construction Vehicles
Two builders and rebuildable site vehicles — workers load, drive, swap trucks. Best value pick; child supplies the destination.

### 4. PLAYMOBIL JUNIOR Take Along Noah's Ark
Portable whole scene with handle — board, travel, arrive. Argos 4.875/5 (16 reviews). Clips stiff; dry play recommended.

### 5. Happyland Farm Tractor
Load cow on trailer, drive to fields, unload — farm **transport** not a whole farm base. Skip if a Happyland farm with tractor already lives at home.

- **Playmobil Junior Farm Adventure** — £50 tier, thin reviews
- **Sylvanian Families nursery bus** — finer parts; beaten by Happyland/DUPLO
- **Schleich figure-only lines** — premium import without UK value beat
- **Fisher-Price Little People farm** — single-location care; bring back out with a vehicle instead

## Borrow / bring back out / hold off guidance

- **Bring back out** kitchen or doll figures as passengers before buying new people.
- **Buy** one missing **theme** (bus route, rescue, site run, portable scene, farm load) — not a second kitchen or mega base.
- **Pre-loved** Happyland and DUPLO are strong on Vinted/eBay — test sounds and hitches.
- **Hold off** on huge sets or premium imports unless nothing similar is already in the house.

## QA concerns before import
