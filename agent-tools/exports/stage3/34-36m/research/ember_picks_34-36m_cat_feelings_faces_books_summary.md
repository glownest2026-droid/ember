# Pip's Picks: Feelings and friendship books (34–36 months)

**Research date:** 2026-07-21 | **Researcher:** cursor | **Schema:** ember_picks_research_v2

## Educational shift

At nearly three, friend play gets **messier**: third children, toy grabs, and lingering grudges. This band needs stories about **sharing, turn-taking, feeling left out and saying sorry** — not basic emotion labelling (28–30m) or "tell me your version" worry books (31–33m).

## What we skipped and why

- **The Colour Monster** — emotion labelling; 28–30m band
- **Being Me** — KS2 poetry anthology
- **The Invisible String** — parent separation, not peer friendship
- **Find Your Happy / What If, Pig?** — general feelings/worry, weak on friend-play conflict
- **When Sadness Comes to Call** — sadness metaphor, no sharing/sorry arc
- **Ruby's Worry** — 31–33m worry-retelling band overlap

## Borrow / bring back out / hold off guidance

- **Borrow first** on Pip & Posy and Food Group if you already own one title in the series.
- **Bring back out** basic feelings labellers from earlier bands for vocabulary — pair with one friendship-conflict pick from this list.
- **Hold off** buying Colour Monster again if it is already on the shelf.
