# Pip's Picks: First jigsaw puzzles (34–36 months)

## Educational shift
Nearly-three is moving from peg posting to **turn, compare, try again**. Peg boards and 2-piece warm-ups are cupboard staples — this card is for real interlocking jigsaws (roughly 3–12 pieces) where the picture stays visible while they work.

## Top 5 Ember Picks
1. **First Farm Friends** — Best for first 12-piece plan — £7.92 — [Amazon UK](https://www.amazon.co.uk/Orchard-Toys-292-Friends-Puzzles/dp/B08R6BWGX1)
2. **First Jungle Friends** — Best for jungle theme fans — £8.50 — [Orchard Toys](https://www.orchardtoys.com/buy/first-jungle-friends-jigsaw-puzzles_496.htm)
3. **On The Farm My First** — Best for warm-up sessions — £5.82 — [Amazon UK](https://www.amazon.co.uk/Ravensburger-First-Puzzle-Jigsaw-Puzzles/dp/B008IZHSEA) *(use 4–5 piece puzzles only)*
4. **Bigjigs Safari 6-piece** — Best for wooden durability — £5.99 — [Wooden Toy Shop](https://www.woodentoyshop.co.uk/products/safari-6-piece-puzzles-3-puzzles)
5. **Rescue Squad** — Best for vehicle quick wins — £6.50 — [Amazon UK](https://www.amazon.co.uk/Orchard-Toys-Rescue-Squad-Jigsaw/dp/B0007TXTJS)

## Ranked longlist summary
Ranks 6–15 hold theme swaps (Dino Friends), progressive boxes (Galt farm, Peter Rabbit), and wooden/vehicle backups. **Big Wheels** and **Galt 4-in-a-box** stay on the longlist — honest **3+** marks fail the strictest 34–36m age overlap.

## Best substitutes if unavailable
- Farm Friends → Jungle Friends (rank 2)
- Jungle Friends → Farm Friends (rank 1)
- Ravensburger warm-up → Rescue Squad (rank 5)
- Safari wood → Big Movers (rank 11)

## What we skipped and why
Five skips: peg/knob puzzles, 50-piece Orchard dinosaur floor puzzle, Giant Road layout puzzle — wrong mechanism or wrong stage.

## Borrow / bring back out / hold off guidance
Borrow or buy pre-loved with a piece count. Bring back out peg puzzles before buying. Hold off 20+ piece sets until 12-piece feels easy.
