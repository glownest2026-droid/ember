# Pip's Picks: Feelings and friendship books (34–36 months)

**Research date:** 2026-07-22 | **Schema:** ember_picks_research_v3 | **Status:** pending-ff-check

## Educational shift

At nearly three, friend play is still messy — but words can help. This card is **not** another feelings chart (28–30m) or worry-retelling (31–33m). It names jealousy, sharing rows, grudges and sorry in **peer play**.

## Top 5 Ember Picks

1. **On Sudden Hill** — three-way jealousy when a new friend joins box-play (*borrow first*).
2. **Pip and Posy: The New Friend** — best friend plays with someone else at the beach (*borrow*).
3. **The Sour Grape** — staying cross after small upsets; funny forgiveness (*borrow*).
4. **Can You Share, Little Whale?** — fresh 2024 group sharing story (*buy if library queue is long*).
5. **That's (Not) Mine** — shouty mine fights that end in shared play (*borrow*).

## What we skipped and why

- **The Colour Monster / In My Heart** — bring back out for vocabulary, not fresh buys for this card.
- **The Invisible String** — parent separation, not peer play.
- **Find Your Happy / What If, Pig? / When Sadness Comes to Call** — mood or worry, not turn-taking.
- **Ruby's Worry** — 31–33m band worry book.

## Borrow / bring back out / hold off guidance

- **Borrow first** on Pip & Posy and Food Group staples — nurseries already own them.
- **Bring back out** 28–30m feelings labellers for vocabulary; pair with one friendship-conflict pick.
- **Hold off** buying another abstract feelings book until friend-play stories are on the shelf.
