## Educational shift

At nearly three, busy hands move from grab-and-post to **neater threading and copying simple patterns**. This band needs **chunky 3+ pieces with supervision** — not jewellery-scale beads. Good gift category if the set looks intentional and stores neatly.

- **Galt Sparkle Jewellery** — 120 small beads, 5+
- **164+ bead jewellery kits** — wrong category entirely
- **Fuse bead ironing kits** — tiny beads + heat hazard
- **Rekenrek abacus** — counting toy, not lacing

## Borrow / bring back out / hold off guidance

- **Borrow** nursery giant tubs before buying bulk sets.
- **Bring back out** any 2+ lacing set with shorter cords and pattern games.
- **Hold off** on jewellery kits until 5+ and sustained fine control.

## Safety notes (category-wide)

- **3+ only** — all picks carry small-parts warnings.
- **Supervise** threading; shorten or remove laces when finished.
- **Reject** tiny bead / jewellery kits (e.g. 164-bead packs, rice beads).
- Keep sets away from younger siblings and tidy beads away after play.
