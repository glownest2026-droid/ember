## Educational shift

Nearly three is no longer “name the feeling.” Friend play gets messy: jealousy when a third joins, snatching, sorry, turn-taking. Picks model peer conflict and repair — not Colour Monster jars or 4–8 reading-age early readers.

## Borrow / bring back out / hold off guidance

Borrow Pip & Posy before stacking the series. Bring these out after a rough playdate — one short read, not a lecture. Hold Colour Monster and 4–8 early readers for other moments.
