# Pip’s Picks: First-word picture books (16–18 months)

## Educational shift
Versus earlier face-only board books: now the job is pointing at a clear word page and hearing the name.

## Top Ember Picks
1. **First 100 Words** (Priddy Books) — Best for photo first words
   - https://priddybooks.com/gb/books/first-100-words/
2. **Baby Touch: Words** (Ladybird) — Best for touch and name
   - https://uk.bookshop.org/p/books/baby-touch-words-ladybird/9780241361634
3. **Baby's First Words** (Barefoot Books) — Best for illustrated first words
   - https://barefootbooks.com/uk/babys-first words/
4. **My First Words: Let's Get Talking** (DK) — Best for talk along first words
   - https://uk.bookshop.org/p/books/my-first words-let-s-get-talking-dk/9780241533376
5. **See, Touch, Feel: First Words** (Priddy Books) — Best for textured first words
   - https://priddybooks.com/gb/books/see-touch-feel-first words/
6. **Baby Touch: Animals** (Ladybird) — Best for animal word pointing
   - https://uk.bookshop.org/p/books/baby-touch-animals-a-black and white-book-ladybird/9780241388129
7. **Dear Zoo** (Rod Campbell) — Best for flap and name
   - https://uk.bookshop.org/p/books/dear-zoo-rod-campbell/9781407030678
8. **Where's Spot?** (Eric Hill) — Best for seek and name
   - https://www.penguin.co.uk/books/122792/wheres-spot-by-hill-eric/9780723263661
9. **The Very Hungry Caterpillar** (Eric Carle) — Best for food word naming
   - https://uk.bookshop.org/p/books/the-very-hungry-caterpillar-eric-carle/9780241003008
10. **That's Not My Puppy** (Fiona Watt) — Best for touch texture naming
   - https://uk.bookshop.org/p/books/that-s-not-my-puppy-fiona-watt/9780746047866
