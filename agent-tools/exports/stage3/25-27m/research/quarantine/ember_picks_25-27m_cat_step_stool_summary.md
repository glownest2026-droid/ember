# Pip’s Picks: A steady step for helping (25–27 months)

ownership_class: **single** · Top 5 / longlist 15

## Educational shift
Just past two, children insist on doing handwashing and toothbrush jobs themselves. A low step beats a tall learning tower for this band.

## Top Ember Picks
1. **BabyBjörn Step Stool** (BabyBjörn) — Best low steady step
   - https://www.babybjorn.co.uk/products/bathroom/step-stool/
   - A low plastic step about fifteen centimetres high with a full non-slip top and rubber feet, light enough for a just past two to drag to the sink.
   - Why Pip: I want to do it myself peaks hard just past two: handwashing and toothbrush jobs they insist on owning. A low, grippy step reaches the basin without a tall learning tower. Steady daily help at the sink when they want to climb up and join in.
2. **FÖRSIKTIG children's stool** (IKEA) — Best simple value step
   - https://www.ikea.com/gb/en/p/foersiktig-childrens-stool-white-turquoise-10591318/
   - A simple IKEA children’s stool with a wide curved base, grip under the feet and a non-slip top, about thirteen centimetres high for basin reach.
   - Why Pip: Not every first step needs a premium brand once they want to wash hands alone. Wide base and low height still cover sink and toilet jobs. A simple spare for bathroom or kitchen when you want a second step upstairs.
3. **Bumbo Step 'n' Potty** (Bumbo) — Best step and potty combo
   - https://www.boots.com/bumbo-step-n-potty
   - A combined step stool and potty trainer with a removable bowl and a lid that becomes a step, so sink reach and potty practice share one footprint.
   - Why Pip: Independence jobs stack just past two: step up to wash, then sit to practise. One unit that flips between step and potty saves bathroom clutter in a small flat. Useful when space is tight and both jobs are starting together in the same bathroom.
4. **TROGEN children’s step stool** (IKEA) — Best handled step stool
   - https://www.ikea.com/gb/en/p/trogen-childrens-step-stool-yellow-80371520/
   - A taller children’s step stool with side handles and a steady base, so they can carry it to the kitchen counter or bathroom and step up to help.
   - Why Pip: Some jobs need a slightly higher step once they help at the counter as well as the sink. Handles let them ferry the stool themselves without asking you to carry it. Good when sink reach is sorted and kitchen helping is the next daily ask.
5. **BabyBjörn Toilet Training Seat** (BabyBjörn) — Best toilet seat helper
   - https://www.babybjorn.co.uk/products/bathroom/toilet-training-seat/
   - A soft, adjustable seat that sits on the adult toilet with a secure fit, so a just past two can practise the big toilet with a step beside them.
   - Why Pip: Once the low step is sorted, some children want the adult toilet next. A child seat on the big toilet pairs with the step for the full bathroom job. Second BabyBjörn bathroom piece when the step is already earning its keep beside the basin every day.
