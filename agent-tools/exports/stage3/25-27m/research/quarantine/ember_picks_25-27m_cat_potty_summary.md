# Pip’s Picks: A potty to practise sitting (25–27 months)

ownership_class: **single** · Top 5 / longlist 15

## Educational shift
Just past two, many children notice potty signs and want to copy the toilet. Practice sitting comes before dry days.

## Top Ember Picks
1. **BabyBjörn Potty Chair** (BabyBjörn) — Best supported potty chair
   - https://www.babybjorn.co.uk/products/bathroom/potty-chair/
   - A high back potty chair with armrests, a removable inner potty and a rubber strip underneath so it stays put while they climb on and off.
   - Why Pip: Noticing potty signs is quieter than full training: they want to sit and copy. A chair with a back and arms lets them settle without wobbling on a tippy bowl. Supported practice sitting when interest is new and you want a steady first seat.
2. **LOCKIG children potty** (IKEA) — Best simple value potty
   - https://www.ikea.com/gb/en/p/lockig-childrens-potty-white-turquoise-40591581/
   - A comfy children potty with a removable insert and non-slip base, easy to empty and wipe clean after each try.
   - Why Pip: Early potty practice does not need a premium chair. A simple bowl with a lift out insert keeps cleaning quick after short sits at home. A plain starter when you want to try sitting practice without a big bathroom purchase this month.
3. **Bumbo Step 'n' Potty** (Bumbo) — Best step and potty combo
   - https://www.boots.com/bumbo-step-n-potty
   - A combined step stool and potty with a removable bowl and a lid that becomes a step for sink reach after sitting practice.
   - Why Pip: Potty practice and sink help often arrive together just past two. One footprint that flips between sit and step saves clutter in a small bathroom at home. Useful when both jobs are starting and floor space is already full of other kit.
4. **BabyBjörn Toilet Training Seat** (BabyBjörn) — Best toilet seat helper
   - https://www.babybjorn.co.uk/products/bathroom/toilet-training-seat/
   - A soft adjustable seat that sits on the adult toilet with a secure fit, so they can practise the big toilet with a step beside them.
   - Why Pip: Once the potty is a known daily sit, some children want the adult toilet. A child seat on the big toilet is the next sitting job with a steady step nearby for climbing. Second BabyBjorn bathroom piece when the chair is already in use downstairs every day.
5. **My Carry Potty Dino** (My Carry Potty) — Best travel potty
   - https://www.boots.com/my-carry-potty-dino-10285341
   - A sealed travel potty with a carry handle and splash safe lid, shaped like a dinosaur so toilet stops away from home stay familiar.
   - Why Pip: Days out break potty practice just when it is getting going at home. A sealed carry potty keeps the same sitting job available in the car or at grandparents. Handy when home practice is started and trips need the same familiar seat.
