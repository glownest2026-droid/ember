# Pip’s Picks: Chunky puzzles and shape sorters (25–27 months)

ownership_class: **single** · Top 5 / longlist 15

## Educational shift
Just past two, matching shapes and lifting chunky pieces gets more deliberate than earlier banging play.

## Top Ember Picks
1. **UPPSTA shape sorter** (IKEA) — Best birdhouse shape sorter
   - https://www.ikea.com/gb/en/p/uppsta-shape-sorter-multicolour-90592088/
   - A birdhouse style wooden shape sorter with chunky coloured blocks that drop through matching holes and rattle inside the house.
   - Why Pip: Busy hands just past two want to match a shape and try again when it sticks. A clear birdhouse sorter keeps that try visible without tiny fiddly pieces. Best first sorter when they are past pure banging and into posting shapes on the floor.
2. **UPPSTA building beakers** (IKEA) — Best stacking sort beakers
   - https://www.ikea.com/gb/en/p/uppsta-building-beakers-multicolour-70513884/
   - A set of colourful nesting and stacking beakers for sort, stack, tip and rebuild play together on the living room floor.
   - Why Pip: Shape work is not only posting through holes. Nesting beakers let them sort by size and tip them out for another go on the floor. Second IKEA busy hands set when the sorter is already on the shelf at home.
3. **Melissa and Doug Shape Sorting Cube** (Melissa & Doug) — Best classic wooden sorting cube
   - https://www.smythstoys.com/uk/en-gb/toys/preschool/educational-toys/melissa-and-doug-shape-sorting-cube/p/159210
   - A classic wooden cube with chunky coloured shapes that post through matching holes on each face of the sturdy box.
   - Why Pip: A wooden cube sorter is the familiar UK shelf staple for matching shapes by hand at this age. Chunky pieces fit just past two better than tiny plastic sets. Good when you want a sturdy wooden cube instead of a birdhouse form.
4. **Melissa and Doug Chunky Pets Puzzle** (Melissa & Doug) — Best chunky farm puzzle
   - https://www.smythstoys.com/uk/en-gb/toys/preschool/educational-toys/melissa-and-doug-chunky-puzzle-pets/p/186194
   - A wooden chunky puzzle with large lift out pet pieces that little hands can grip, lift and seat again on the board.
   - Why Pip: Shape sorting is one busy hands job; lift out puzzles are the next step for just past two. Chunky pet pieces let them lift, name and reseat without tiny tabs. Second Melissa and Doug slot when the cube is already sorted.
5. **Galt Toys Chunky Farm Puzzle** (Galt Toys) — Best Galt chunky farm puzzle
   - https://www.smythstoys.com/uk/en-gb/toys/preschool/educational-toys/galt-toys-chunky-farm-puzzle/p/159212
   - A colourful chunky farm puzzle with large wooden pieces for lift, match and reseat play together on the floor mat.
   - Why Pip: A second chunky farm puzzle brand keeps ownership flexible if Melissa and Doug is already on the shelf. Large pieces fit busy hands just past two. Good gift when you know they already enjoy lift out animal pieces at home.
