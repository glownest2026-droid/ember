# Pip’s Picks: A play kitchen for everyday copying (25–27 months)

ownership_class: **single** · Top 5 / longlist 15

## Educational shift
Just past two, copying toast, pots and chopping gets more deliberate. Freestanding kitchens and many soft-food sets marked from 3 years wait; prefer 18m+ tabletop props when UK buyable.

## Top Ember Picks
1. **Bigjigs Toys Wooden Breakfast Set** (Bigjigs Toys) — Best for everyday toast copy
   - https://www.jarrolds.co.uk/departments/toys-and-hobbies/preschool-and-infant/roleplay-and-dressing-up/bigjigs-toys-breakfast-set
   - A wooden toaster breakfast set with toast, butter, jam and a knife, sized for short everyday toast copy on the floor or table.
   - Why Pip: Just past two, toast copy is the kitchen job that comes before a full freestanding set. Push the lever, wait for the pop, then spread. Start here when you want a short breakfast copy they can join without waiting for a three years kitchen.
2. **Bigjigs Toys Wooden Pots and Pans Set** (Bigjigs Toys) — Best for pots and stirring
   - https://www.woodentoyshop.co.uk/products/wooden-pots-and-pans-set
   - A seven piece wooden pots and pans set with lids and utensils for stirring and serving copy beside the family cooker.
   - Why Pip: Once toast copy is known, stirring and lid play keeps the kitchen job going without a tall freestanding unit. Hold a pot, wait for a stir, then serve. Second Bigjigs only when the breakfast set already earns floor time at home.
3. **Tidlo Wooden Cutting Fruits Set** (Tidlo) — Best for chop and share fruit
   - https://www.woodentoyshop.co.uk/products/cutting-fruits-set
   - A wooden cutting fruits set with tray and knife so they can chop one fruit piece and share it on a plate.
   - Why Pip: Just past two, chopping copy needs a safe knife and fruit pieces that come apart. Cut one piece, wait for a share, then start again. Good when toast and pots are already known and you want a chopping job at the table.
4. **Tender Leaf Toys Breakfast Toaster Set** (Tender Leaf Toys) — Best for healthy toast toppings
   - https://www.jarrolds.co.uk/departments/toys-and-hobbies/preschool-and-infant/roleplay-and-dressing-up/tender-leaf-toys-breakfast-toaster-set
   - A wooden toaster breakfast set with egg and avocado toppings, listed from three years on the UK retailer page today.
   - Why Pip: This toaster set looks like the right everyday job, but the listing marks three years plus. Hold for a later band unless an eighteen months edition appears on a UK host. Kept here so the age wall stays visible beside the Bigjigs breakfast set.
5. **Hape Pop-up Toaster Set** (Hape) — Best for brand toaster spare
   - https://www.adventuretoys.co.uk/hape-toaster-pop-up-set/
   - A wooden pop up toaster set with toast, butter and honey pieces, sold in the UK with an Age 3 plus mark.
   - Why Pip: Another buyable UK toaster set that still carries a three years mark on the host page. Hold until the band clears thirty six months or a younger age mark appears. Kept so founder review sees the age wall beside the breakfast pick.
