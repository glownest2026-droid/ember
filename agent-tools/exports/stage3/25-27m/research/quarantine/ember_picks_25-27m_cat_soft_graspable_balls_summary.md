# Pip’s Picks: Soft balls to kick and chase (25–27 months)

ownership_class: **single** · Top 5 / longlist 15

## Educational shift
Just past two, kicking and chasing get more deliberate. Soft balls keep indoor practice kind to shins and furniture.

## Top Ember Picks

   - A small soft fabric football filled for gentle kicks and cuddles, sized for little hands and kind to shins indoors.
   - Why Pip: Confident moving just past two means kick, chase and bring it back. A mini soft football keeps that game indoors without a hard ball on the rug. Best first soft kick about when they want to join the match after breakfast.
