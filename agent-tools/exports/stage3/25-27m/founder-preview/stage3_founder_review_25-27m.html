HOW — ranking trail by category

Use this when asking “why #1?” or “why these five?”. Answers must come from these fields, not invented chat.

Bedtime books with familiar steps

Keep curious days safer

Buying-factor memo

Familiar steps first (ten minutes, goodnight round, going to bed sequence). Then love-you closers. Brand cap two per author. High ownership risk on UK staples. Specialist ratings path after Amazon bot walls.
