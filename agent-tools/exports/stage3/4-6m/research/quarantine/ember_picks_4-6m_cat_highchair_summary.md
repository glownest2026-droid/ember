# Pip’s Picks: A supportive highchair (4–6 months)

## Educational shift
Chair wait until sitting unaided; tastes approach at the end of 4–6m.

## Top 5 Ember Picks
1. **IKEA ANTILOP Highchair seat shell** — Best for budget wipe-clean
   - The IKEA ANTILOP highchair seat shell, completed with separate legs and tray, giving a simple wipe-clean seat once sitting without help is reliable.
   - Why Pip: First tastes are getting close, but the chair only belongs when sitting is steady without help. ANTILOP keeps cleanup honest for porridge experiments. Budget for legs and tray as well as the shell on the same trip so you leave with a usable seat.
2. **Joie Mimzy Snacker Highchair** — Best for compact fold storage
   - A Joie Mimzy Snacker highchair with a simple fold, adjustable tray and storage basket for compact homes preparing for first table meals.
   - Why Pip: When ANTILOP feels too bare, Mimzy adds fold and tray storage for small kitchens. Confirm live stock on Boots before ordering because retailer pages can soft block bots. Strong compact fold candidate in UK roundups for flats and narrow dining spaces.
3. **Stokke Tripp Trapp Highchair** — Best for wooden grow chair
   - The Stokke Tripp Trapp wooden chair that grows from highchair use with the Baby Set into a lifelong dining seat at the family table.
   - Why Pip: If you want one chair for years, Tripp Trapp is the UK premium answer, with Baby Set extras in the budget. Not the first buy if weaning is weeks away and cash is tight. Confirm live Boots stock before you commit to the full set.
4. **Cosatto Noodle 0 Highchair Mister Fox** — Best for patterned wipe chair
   - A Cosatto Noodle 0 highchair in Mister Fox print with a wipe-clean seat and compact footprint for early supported mealtimes at home.
   - Why Pip: When you want more colour than ANTILOP without Tripp Trapp money, Noodle is the UK pattern pick. Confirm Boots stock live; soft bot pages appeared during research. Still a clear chair for the sitting stage once independent sitting is reliable.
5. **Bugaboo Giraffe High Chair** — Best for design table seat
   - A Bugaboo Giraffe high chair aimed at bringing babies up to the family table with a clean modular frame once sitting is steady.
   - Why Pip: More design led and pricier; only if the look and longevity come ahead of the budget path for a table seat. Confirm Boots stock live before ordering. Ranked last because ANTILOP, Mimzy and Tripp Trapp cover most Conor needs first for early tastes.
