# Pip’s Picks: A clean floor-play mat (4–6 months)

## Educational shift
At 4–6m tummy spells last longer and reaching starts; the floor is a little gym, not a newborn stretch pad.

## Top 5 Ember Picks
1. **The Play Gym** — Best for longer tummy sessions
   - A washable organic cotton floor gym with a wooden frame, hanging toys and a play guide that moves from short tummy spells into reaching and rolling on the living room floor.
   - Why Pip: By four to six months the floor becomes a proper workout space, not a quick stretch on a towel. This gym keeps tummy time interesting with reachables that change as rolling starts. The washable mat survives milk dribbles without turning the room into a plastic play zone for every awake spell.
2. **The Mariner Playmat** — Best for wipe-clean living mats
   - A reversible Totter and Tumble floor mat with a soft cushioned core and a wipe-clean surface, sized so you can sit beside them for longer tummy sessions without hard boards underneath.
   - Why Pip: Once tummy time lasts more than a minute, cold floors punish elbows and knees. A wipe-clean cushioned mat lets you join them on the floor without a giant foam puzzle. Reversible faces keep it looking intentional when visitors walk through after lunch on a busy day.
3. **Rainbow Playmat Set in Cream** — Best for modular foam patches
   - A thick non-toxic EVA foam puzzle playmat in a simple cream rainbow design, so you can build a soft patch on hard floors and pull tiles apart after spills.
   - Why Pip: Hard tiles punish longer tummy spells once they push up and rock. Puzzle foam softens the floor without covering the whole room in one roll. Pull one tile aside after a spill and the rest of the patch stays ready for the next try on the same afternoon.
4. **Plain Playmat Set in Navy, Sage and Cream** — Best for plain expandable tiles
   - A Gus and Beau plain EVA foam tile set in navy, sage and cream that expands with extra tiles, giving a soft patch for tummy time on hard floors without busy prints.
   - Why Pip: Busy prints can feel loud once the mat stays down all day. Plain tiles keep the floor soft while the room still looks like a living room. Add tiles later if rolling turns into crawling and the soft patch needs to grow with them.
5. **The Ludo Playmat** — Best for alternate Totter prints
   - A Totter and Tumble Ludo reversible wipe-clean playmat with the same soft cushion as Mariner, offered in a different double sided print for the main floor zone.
   - Why Pip: Some parents want the Totter wipe-clean feel but not the Mariner colours. Ludo keeps the same cushion for longer tummy sessions as rolling starts. Pick one Totter mat only; two large mats in the same home usually just fight for floor space.

## What we skipped and why
- DockATot-style loungers used as play mats: Wrong category — contained loungers are not open floor gym mats.
- Temu generic foam mats: Excluded marketplace source; identity and safety unclear.
- Adult yoga mats only: Too thin and slippery for baby elbow load during tummy time.
- Cot bumpers / sleep nests: Sleep products are not floor-play mats; safety conflict.
- US-only Lovevery.com checkout: Non-UK primary; use lovevery.co.uk instead.
