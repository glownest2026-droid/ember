## Guidance Notes

- Many high-contrast card sets are sold on Amazon UK by smaller brands or international sellers with varying levels of detail in their product descriptions and review counts. Prioritize products with clear UK availability and strong, verifiable retail proof (4.4+ rating with 50+ reviews).
- Some Etsy listings are for digital downloads or from shops that are temporarily closed/sold out. Focus on physical products from active sellers with good reviews.
- Consider the material and durability of the cards, as babies in this age range will often interact with them physically. Wipeable and sturdy cards are preferable.
- For products offering 'staged' development (e.g., 0-3 months, then 3-6 months), ensure the 0-3 month component is clearly high-contrast black and white, as per the category's primary focus.
