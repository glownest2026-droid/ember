## Guidance Notes

- Many high-quality infant car seats require a separate ISOFIX base for optimal safety and ease of use, which can significantly increase the overall cost. It is crucial to clearly communicate this to parents to manage expectations and ensure they factor in the full expense. Additionally, emphasize the importance of checking car seat compatibility with their specific vehicle model before purchase, as not all seats fit all cars, even with ISOFIX.
